Kashmir can be rightly termed as "Heaven on Earth," famous for its breathtaking landscapes, tranquil lakes, and green valleys, all of which make this place one of the most breathtaking places in the world. There are several places of special regard among the many wonders that Kashmir boasts; these three stand out for their distinct beauty and cultural essence. These include Gulmarg, Srinagar, and Sonamarg. While these cities offer breathtaking natural beauty, they are also connected with the rich history and traditions that this region stands for. Let's dig deeper to find out what makes each of these places a must-see for any and every person who is eyeing Kashmir to have a really great time.
